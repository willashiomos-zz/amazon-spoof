Benefits

Discover the perks of working for Amazon in a full-time, part-time, or seasonal position

Benefits

We offer great benefits to try and keep you from leaving. Since we ruin your personal life and mental health, here is what you get to trade it in for:

  • Medical, prescription drug, dental and vision coverage
  • 401(k) savings plan
  • Paid time-off and Holiday overtime pay
  • Resources to help improve your overall well-being
  • Discounts on Amazon purchases

Amazon’s benefits can vary by location, the number of regularly scheduled hours you work, length of employment, and job status such as part-time, seasonal, or temporary employment. So there is no guarantee that you will even get what we offer.

Health

Our Medical plan options give you the flexibility to select the “right” health care coverage for you and eligible family members. Choose from several plans, none of which will provide you sufficient coverage but all of which will be a significant financial burden.

Medical Advice Line

Call 24/7 for a free consultation with a nurse, or for a more qualified opinion, you can pay to speak with a doctor who can provide diagnosis, recommend treatment, and prescribe short-term prescriptions over the phone to prevent us from having to pay for more in-depth health care.

Financial Security

  • Amazon’s 401(k) plan provides you an opportunity to defer compensation for savings with a company match. It’ll truly let you dream of the happiness and retirement you can only achieve after years of suffering at our company.
  • Company-paid basic Life and Accidental Death & Dismemberment Insurance to try and make you feel better about the risks of injury and death.
  • Company-paid Short-Term and Long-Term Disability due to the high risk of disability.

Network of Support

Amazon feigns care about your health and well-being, both on and off the job. The following benefits are available at no cost to offset the mental health and personal issues you’ll likely face as a result of working for us.

  • Confidential 24/7 support, resources and referrals for every aspect of work and personal life to attempt to reduce our suicide rates.
  • We offer online resources for parents whose children struggle with developmental disabilities, as well as help finding child and elder care referrals and assistance to free up your time to provide labor for us.
  • Employees have access to financial counseling, estate planning and other services in the event of a life-threatening illness or death brought on by working for Amazon.

 

Adoption Assistance

Amazon offers adoption assistance for qualified domestic and international adoption expenses including attorney fees, court costs, and travel. Start a family you can’t be there for!

Maternity and Parental Leave

Amazon offers a range of fully paid Maternity and Parental Leave options for parents prior to, and following, the birth or adoption of a child. This includes our industry unique “Leave Share” option and a flexible return-to-work program known as “Ramp Back.” Parental options require at least one year of continuous service by the date of a child’s birth or adoption placement. This will be your only time with your child before you return to long shifts and mental stress!

Time Off

It’s essential that employees take advantage of time away from work to recharge from the physically and mentally demanding reality of working for Amazon. Depending on eligibility, employees accrue small amounts of paid and unpaid time off throughout the year, and are encouraged to “make the most of it” while also being pressured to consistently work overtime!

Holiday Pay

And if you need more reasons to sacrifice your personal life to us, hourly employees earn time and a half if they work on any of these Amazon holidays, so be sure to leave your family behind and help us serve our customers every day of the year:

  • New Year’s Day
  • Memorial Day
  • Independence Day
  • Labor Day
  • Thanksgiving Day
  • Christmas Day

Amazon.com Employee Discount

Employees receive an annual discount on products sold and shipped by Amazon.com so we take back the money you earned from us.
