How we're responding to the crisis

Masks for employees

Amazon has been busy finding creative solutions to ensure efficiency is maintained during the global pandemic. The new distant assistant will soon be deployed to our biggest fulfillment centers across the country. It utilizes camera and AI technology to track our associates while they work to ensure they stay six feet apart. This autonomous surveillance will allow us to track and monitor our employees more effectively for covid and strip them of any privacy and freewill.

Social distancing

Due to the strain on our supply chain from customers, Amazon can only offer one disposable mask to each associate a day. We encourage everyone to take and use them, but they are not required! In addition to masks, Amazon has also added plexiglass boxes around each manager, bringing them closer to becoming the no-life robots we see them as!

 

Temperature checks

Amazon has turned our warehouse classrooms, previously used for employee education programs, into a testing center for temperature checks and covid screening. Education isn’t mandatory for the repetitive work employees will be tasked with.

What is Amazon doing to provide a safe environment for employees at this time?

Amazon has been committed to controlling the spread of the virus. Throughout the several past months, we have taken steps to eliminate all human contact and socialization ensures none of our associates are able to come in contact with one another, furthering their sense of isolation on the job and increasing their efficiency!

I’m laying off my employees because of COVID-19, are there temporary jobs until I can hire them back?

Due to the pandemic, Amazon needs workers more than ever. If your company is struggling to stay open, Amazon can provide temporary work for employees and employers until things return to normal. Amazon needs this support during these tough times to ensure Jeff Bezos doesn’t have to worry about loss of income.

Can I work for Amazon if there is a Curfew/Shelter-in-Place order in my city?

Yes, due to the scale of Amazon it remains open as an essential service. By working for Amazon during this pandemic, you will be able to risk your life in the warehouse while others stay safe at home. You will be delivering and packing supplies directly to serve the non-essential customers of the world, who we value above all else for their countless purchases from our site.
